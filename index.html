Looking for the Perfect Preschool?

Consider CHALK of Manhattan Beach/California.

CHALK Preschool of Manhattan Beach is located at 1114 22nd St. in Manhattan Beach, California. There are two separate buildings situated on this corner lot property that enclosse a spacious outdoor gross motor area. Each of our classrooms open up to an attached outdoor area that will provide the quintessential southern California indoor/outdoor learning environment. This secure campus of 15,000 square feet is sure to stimulate curiosity and nurture a love for learning.

What we offer…

  • CHALK Preschool offers 2, 3 or 5 Full or Half Day programs for children ages 2-6 (Children do not have to be toilet trained.) Choice of days if attending 2 or 3 full days (based on availability.)
  • Child-centered learning activities.
  • Developmentally and age appropriate materials and equipment.
  • Two teachers in each classroom holding degrees in Early Childhood or a related field.
  • The #1 ranking My Gym children’s fitness program- equipment, materials and curriculum.
  • Faculty that receive continuing education and training to promote on-going professional development.
  • Effective family/teacher communication.
  • First Aid and CPR certified faculty.
  • Opportunities for parents to be involved in their student’s education.
  • Open door policy for parents to contact all administrators.
  • Secure entrances and exits.

CHALK teachers provide the vital inspiration to students as they discover the world around them. The teachers and staff focus all activities and experiences on the children. CHALK seeks to support the development of the whole child across emotional, social, physical, and cognitive domains.

Children will experience moments of awe and wonder every day at CHALK Preschool… Shouldn’t you consider CHALK today?
